**Description:** Configures a setting

**__Usage__**

-p-config

**__Subcommands__**

-p-config lockbypass <role>: Sets a role that can bypass locks
-p-config updatechannel <channel>: Sets the update channel
-p-config logchannel <channel>: Sets the log channel
-p-config modlogs <channel>: Sets the mod log channel
-p-config mutedrole <role|create>: Sets the muted role

**__Premium Only__**

-p-config prefix <prefix>
-p-config verifiedrole <role>
-p-config unverifiedrole <role>
-p-config punishdm <enable|disable>
